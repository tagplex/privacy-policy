Privacy Policy

App Name: IPTV PLAYER

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how IPTV PLAYER handles information on Samsung Tizen Smart TVs. This Application is a generic media player designed to play user-provided content via M3U/M3U8 playlists and other streaming formats.

2. No Data Collection

We believe in absolute privacy. Our policy is simple:

  • No Registration: Users are not required to create an account or provide personal identity information.
  • No Server-Side Storage: We do not operate external servers to store user data, preferences, or usage history.
  • No Analytics: We do not track your behavior or usage patterns within the app.

3. User-Provided Content

The Application functions strictly as a technical tool for playback:

  • Responsibility: All content (playlists, URLs, EPG data) loaded into the Application is the sole responsibility of the user.
  • Local Processing: Any playlist URL entered is processed and stored locally on the Samsung Smart TV memory only. This information is never uploaded or shared.
  • No Content Provided: We do not provide, host, or sell any media content or IPTV subscriptions.

4. Third-Party Links

The Application may play streams hosted by third parties. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these external providers.

5. Security

Since no data is transmitted to us, your data security depends on the local security of your Samsung Smart TV device.
