PRIVACY POLICY FOR IPTV PLAYER Last Updated: January 11, 2026 1. INTRODUCTION This Privacy Policy describes how IPTV PLAYER ("the Application") handles information on Samsung Tizen Smart TVs. This Application is a generic media player designed to play user-provided content via M3U/M3U8 playlists and other streaming formats. 2. NO DATA COLLECTION We believe in absolute privacy. IPTV PLAYER does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No Registration: Users are not required to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal identity information. No Server-Side Storage: We do not operate any external servers to store user data, preferences, or usage history. 3. USER-PROVIDED CONTENT (IPTV LISTS) The Application functions strictly as a technical tool. Responsibility: All content (playlists, URLs, EPG data) loaded into the Application is the sole responsibility of the user. Local Processing: Any playlist URL or link entered by the user is processed and stored locally on the Samsung Smart TV device memory only for the purpose of functioning. This information is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties. No Content Provided: IPTV PLAYER does not provide, host, or sell any media content or IPTV subscriptions. 4. THIRD-PARTY LINKS The Application may play streams hosted by third parties. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these external providers. We recommend users ensure they have the legal right to access any stream they load into the player. 5. SECURITY Since no data is transmitted to us, the security of your local data depends on the security of your Samsung Smart TV device. We recommend keeping your TV firmware updated. 6. COMPLIANCE This policy is designed to comply with global privacy standards, including GDPR and CCPA, by virtue of not collecting any personal data. 7.
